Early years
Dhananjai Singh was born on 4th May, 1986 in Jaipur. He is the first child of Gajendra Singh and Priti Kumari, who hail from the erstwhile family of Khimsar. Dhananjai is the 20th direct descendent of Rao Karamsi (Founder of the Karamsot Clan of Rathore's). He completed his school education from Mayo College, Ajmer. In school, he was a keen sportsman and writer. He was adjourned as Best Debater. He was also awarded the Abdul Wajid Stow Medal for excellence. He also achieved excellence in the game of Squash. After winning various Inter school championships, he captained Rajasthan in the Under 19 National Squash Championship. He also participated in several International tournaments and went on to secure 3rd position at the Malaysian International Open in Penang. Thereafter, he went abroad and graduated from the prestigious Ecole Hotelier Glion, Switzerland. He gained work experience in Hotel Blancafort, Barcelona (Spain) and Crown Plaza, Melbourne (Australia). HIs education and work experience abroad helped him to efficiently manage and interact with people of different backgrounds and cultures. A Hotelier by profession, he is the director of the Khimsar Group of Hotels, which has been recipient of the National Grand Heritage Award For Excellence(this being the highest recognition conferred upon any heritage property by the Department of Tourism, Government of India). He got married to Mrigesha Kumari Singh (erstwhile family of Rajkot, Gujarat) on 17th of January, 2012. Dhananjai is widely travelled and has visited USA, Canada, England, Scotland, Ireland, Switzerland, Spain, Italy, Germany, Austria, France, Belgium, Netherlands, Australia, Malaysia, Thailand for various business and social endeavours. Since a tender age, Dhananjai has been actively involved in various social causes. His parents ensured that along with getting quality education he is dedicated to the service of the people, the specially the poor. He actively participates in various social programs and aims to work for the upliftment of his host region. He is also the founder of the Aanadata Charitable Trust. This charitable organisation aims at helping the people of rural Rajasthan overcome hurdles in their daily lives. Dhananjai Singh is a member of the Bhartiya Janta Party and takes active part in politics. He has actively campaigned for the BJP in the past state Assembly elections and participates in various BJP campaigns. His father, Gajendra Singh ji has been elected to the Rajasthan Legislative Assembly for the third time and is the Cabinet Minister for Energy, Govt. of Rajasthan. Gajendra Singh ji also served as State Energy Minister in previous BJP government (2003-2009).
