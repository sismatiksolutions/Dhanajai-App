ONCE UPON A TIME!!!!

Today, in my country, corruption has reached a new low- an impairment and perversion of and integrity, virtues and moral principles.In my country, we let such heinous acts occur time and time again. In my country, there is no fear of breaking the law- courtesy the judicial system.
We have recently witnessed a mammoth anti corruption movement against the political system. Is that the answer to a better India?
The judicial courts are the highest authorities in our country. The judiciary needs to provide immediate justice. Once there is a fear of breaking the law of the land, the system will automatically fall into order. The rot and corruption will subside.
Let us demand for 'Justice in 30 days for the Delhi Braveheart'. Let us ascertain that this case does not linger on for a couple of years.
The call of the hour is anger- channelled in the right direction, for the world often continues to allow evil because it isn't angry enough.
Good morning... I hope everyone is awake !
